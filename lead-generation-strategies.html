Lead Generation Strategies That Work

You’ve worked hard to get traffic to your website. Now it’s time to turn that traffic into leads and customers. Here are five proven strategies I use with my clients.

Watch this overview of effective lead generation tactics.

1. Create a Compelling Lead Magnet

Offer something valuable in exchange for an email address – a free ebook, checklist, or discount code. Make sure it’s directly related to what your audience wants.

2. Use Clear, Action‑Oriented CTAs

Your calls‑to‑action should stand out and tell visitors exactly what to do. “Get Your Free Quote” works better than “Submit.”

3. Optimize Your Forms

Keep forms short (name and email is often enough). Place them above the fold and use trust signals like “We never spam.”

4. Design Dedicated Landing Pages

For each campaign, create a landing page with a single goal. Remove navigation links to keep visitors focused.

5. Add Exit‑Intent Popups

When a user is about to leave, display a targeted offer. Done right, this can capture up to 10% of abandoning visitors.
